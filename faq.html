Not a problem! It’s entirely irrelevant what your experience is going into a hackathon, it’s more about your interest in technology. We make our hackathons very welcoming and beginner-friendly through mentors at the event and our amazing community that is happy to help new folks. If you want to dive into code or sharpen your skills, check out Treehouse, Codecademy, or FreeCodeCamp.

Item 2

Class is every weekday from 9AM - 5PM, with holiday and vacation breaks. We assign daily homework and projects, including over holidays; anticipate 10-15 hours of study outside of the class per week. We also ask that you participate in community events to exemplify our values of community, inclusivity, and lifelong learning.

Hack Upstate’s mission is to unite and facilitate collaboration among the greater Upstate New York technology community. In doing so, we aim to contribute to the growth of Upstate New York’s technology sector, and to create a robust network of technologists and regional technology companies.
