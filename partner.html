Partner With Us

Hiring capable software engineers that are local is really, really hard, but we've got you covered. We're a tech talent pipeline for local organizations just like you. We're here to help you grow.
Coding bootcamps are a proven model. We provide software education and training opportunities to women and minorities from Central NY’s distressed communities and help them get hired with companies like yours.

Hack Upstate’s mission is to unite and facilitate collaboration among the greater Upstate New York technology community. In doing so, we aim to contribute to the growth of Upstate New York’s technology sector, and to create a robust network of technologists and regional technology companies.
